Patamedi

Patamedi Jonathan Lebea. I have more nicknames than I care to remember: Pati, PataPata, PJ, Jono, PataBantwana etc. All of them are fine excpet one. Pat. I hate the name Pat. It's not just that people who hear that name allmost always presume my full name is Patrick whilst the rest jokingly call me Patricia, no. I dislike the name Pat, mostly because the most well known figure with the same name was a postman with a black and white cat, and who's greatest achievement was great service delivery. My name, my real name, means so much more. Patamedi, which means Warrior in kelobedu, was given to me by my father who himself was the son of Jonathan Patamedi Lebea, my grandfather. Patamedi is not just a name but also an identity. I am a warrior in all that I do; I fight to the finish, and the finish will be when I win. I am a creative hybrid who is yet to find a thing they cannot do. Having said that, writing and creative strategy are my greatest abilities in my catalogue of creative superpowers. In simple: I'm a full-time creative, part-time superhero with no time to explain...
